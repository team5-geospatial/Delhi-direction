INTRODUCTION

The analysis reveals significant trends in urban land dynamics, highlighting the importance of geometric assessments in urban planning. North: Strongest increase (~300 units by 2020). East and Northeast: Significant growth, perhaps related to new roads and developments. West & South-West: Moderate growth, demonstrating balanced sprawl. South and South-East: Restricted growth, perhaps due to older city borders or geographic constraints. Trend: Slower expansion in the south, faster growth in the north and east. Planning for sustainable development in high-growth areas while protecting southern regions is one suggestion. (Units most likely indicate percentage change or area in km².)
